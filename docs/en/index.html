Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

MuscleTrace (hereinafter "the App") respects and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

The App stores the following information only on your device:

  • Workout menu information (exercise names, sets, weight, reps, etc.)
  • Training records (date, workout details, etc.)
  • App settings

Important: All of this information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

3. How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Recording and managing your workouts
  • Displaying workout history
  • Providing app functionality

4. Information Sharing

The App does not share any personal information or workout data with third parties. All data is stored exclusively on your device.

5. Data Retention

All data is stored on your device until you uninstall the app or manually delete it.

6. Security

The App utilizes iOS security features to protect your data. Data is stored in an encrypted format on your device.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob (Advertising Service)

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising purposes. AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Data related to ad impressions and clicks

This information is used to display personalized ads and measure advertising effectiveness. For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

On devices running iOS 14.5 or later, App Tracking Transparency (ATT) will prompt you to allow tracking. Even if you do not allow tracking, you can still use the app normally, but the ads displayed may not be personalized.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. If a child under 13 uses the App, parental consent is required.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. Any changes will be announced on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: yun0101youmel@gmail.com

11. Data Deletion

If you wish to delete all data, you can uninstall the app or delete it from within the app settings.
